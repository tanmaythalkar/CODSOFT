About Barcelona

Barcelona is the second largest Spanish city in population and the principal industrial and commercial centre of the country.It is surrounded by mountains with fertile plains and a sandy coast. It is city with a wide range of original leisure option that encourage you to visit time and time again .Overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, and famous for Gaudi and other Art Nouveau architecture, Barcelona is one of Europe's trendiest cities.

Some Cool Stories

The first human settlementcin Barcelona date back to Neothlic times. The city itself was founded by the Romans who set up a colony called Barcino at end of 1st century BC. The colony had some thousand inhabitants and was bounded by a defensive wall,the remains of which can still be seen in the old town.
